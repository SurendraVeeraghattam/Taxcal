User Tax Calculation

As per below Standards user can calculate the tax according to there Earnings or Income of the Perspective year.

Rs.0 to 7 lakh - 0% tax rate

Rs.7 lakh to 9 lakh - 5%

Rs.9 lakh to 12 lakh - 10%

Rs.12 lakh to 15 lakh - 15%

Above Rs.15 lakh - 30%

Standard deduction of Rs 50000
